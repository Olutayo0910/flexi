Frequently Asked Questions

What is FlexiWork? +

FlexiWork is a platform that connects individuals seeking flexible work opportunities with employers looking for skilled talents to complete various tasks and projects.

How do I sign up? +

Signing up is simple! Just click on the "Sign Up" button, fill out the registration form with your details, and choose whether you want to sign up as a talent or an employer.

Is there a fee to use FlexiWork? +

Signing up and creating a profile on FlexiWork is free. However, we may charge a small service fee on transactions made through the platform. More details can be found in our pricing policy.

How can I post a job or task? +

Once you're signed up as an employer, you can post a job or task by logging into your account, navigating to the "Post a Job" section, and filling out the necessary details about the task or job.

How do I find work as a talent? +

After signing up as a talent, you can browse available jobs and tasks on the platform. You can apply to those that match your skills and negotiate terms directly with employers.
